ii.

Nodes & objects

toolkit-novel

For a relational dataset, nodes are deterministic — every table in the schema is a node. Object grouping, descriptions and implied nodes are LLM-inferred and editable: rename objects inline, drag nodes between them, or open a node's detail. Implied nodes are entities the schema implies without a table of their own.

Connections — solid edges are foreign keys; dashed means implied.

readings is the spine: site_id resolves the place into sites, reading_id hangs media off it in attachments. region_code implies a region entity with no table of its own.
