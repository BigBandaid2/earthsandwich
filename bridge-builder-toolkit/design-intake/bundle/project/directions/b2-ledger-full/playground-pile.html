toolkit-novel

For a file dataset, nodes are deterministic — every unique field signature is a node, and referenced media is a node. Node names, object grouping and descriptions are LLM-inferred: rename inline, drag nodes between objects, or open a node's detail. Implied nodes are entities the data implies without a table of their own.

Potential connections — every edge is a candidate join key; dashed means implied.
