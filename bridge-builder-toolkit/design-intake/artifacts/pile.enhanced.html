## ID-candidate columns
- `record_id` - unique across both files, stable prefix-coded (r####/s####); strongest join key.
- `attachment` - unique but derived from record_id; treat as dependent, not identity.

## Entity classification
One row = one field observation captured at a place and time, with optional photo evidence.

## Pipeline-metadata vs original-source fields
- Source: `captured_at`, `note`, `place`, `lat`, `lng`, `attachment`.
- Pipeline-added: `source_file` (toolkit concat marker).

## Likely derived/inferred fields
- `place` may be reverse-geocoded from lat/lng for rows where coordinates exist but prose lacks a location.

## AI-workflow evidence
No explicit model-output columns present; `note` phrasing is human-authored.

## Categorical slicing candidates
- `source_file` (per-feed comparison), presence/absence of coordinates, month of `captured_at`.

## Public-knowledge enrichment
Station coordinates can be cross-checked against OpenStreetMap; place names against GeoNames (geonames.org).
